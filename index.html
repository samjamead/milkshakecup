Welcome to the 2019 Milkshake Cup

Date and Time
11am, Sunday 15th September 2019

Location
Westcountry Golf Academy, Tiverton Parkway, EX16 7EH

Format
Handicapped strokeplay over 18 holes (2 x 9 hole rounds combined).

For golfers, 1/2 Handicap appies.
For non golfers, handicap will be set at 36, therefore 18 on the day.

We will play nine holes, stop for a bite to eat, then play the second nine holes. The draw for the first round groups will be out of the hat. Second round groups will be determined by the score after nine holes. The winner is the golfer with the lowest net score after 18 holes!
